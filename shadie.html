Welcome to Our Community

ETERNAL LIFE KINGDOM HARVEST MINISTRY is a vibrant and welcoming church located in Nairobi, Huruma, opposite of Nikos Supermarket. Our mission is to share the love of Christ and empower lives through His word. We invite you to experience a community where you can grow in faith and find your purpose.

Our church is faithfully led by Apostle Jacob and Pastor Francisca Jacob.

Sunday Service Times

  • Official Service: 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM

Weekly Fellowships & Keshas

  • Weekly Fellowships: Wednesday & Friday, doors are always open.
  • End of Month Kesha: Every last Friday of the month.
  • Ministers' Kesha: Every Saturday for all church ministers.
